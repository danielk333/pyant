FiniteCylindricalParabola¶

class pyant.FiniteCylindricalParabola(azimuth, elevation, frequency, I0, width, height, **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: pyant.beam.Beam

A finite Cylindrical Parabola with a finite receiver line feed in the longitudinal direction, i.e. in the direction of the cylinder.
